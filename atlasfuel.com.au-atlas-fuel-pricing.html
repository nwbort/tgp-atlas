Terms and Conditions

All prices are quoted in Australian dollars per litre and include GST, where applicable.

L15 Terminal Gate Prices are applicable to all Resellers and Wholesalers supplied with Gasoline and Diesel, calculated based on the fuel volume at a temperature of 15 degrees Celsius.

Premium Diesel is available, will incur an additional charges.

Terminal Gate Prices are based on a minimum full tanker load of 35,000 litres.

Payment is required in full via Direct Debit / EFT (real-time gross settlement) prior to the collection of the product.

Competitive delivery rates may be offered upon request.

Vehicles and drivers lifting Atlas Fuel products must meet the company’s accreditation requirements.

In times of supply shortages, Atlas Fuel reserves the right to withhold supply or impose rationing.

Prices are subject to change without prior notice at the discretion of Atlas Fuel.
